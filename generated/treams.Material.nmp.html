treams.Material.nmp¶

property Material.nmp¶

Refractive indices of both helicities.

The refractive indices are defined by \(n_\pm = \sqrt{\epsilon \mu} \pm \kappa\), with an enforced non-negative imaginary part.

Note

The refractive indices returned depend on the chirality parameter. For an alternative definition of the refractive index, see also Material.n().

Returns:

tuple
